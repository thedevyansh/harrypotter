Lead Characters of Harry Potter Series

Albus Dumbledore

He is the headmaster of the wizarding school Hogwarts. He is the founder and leader of the Order of the Phoenix, an organisation dedicated to fighting Lord Voldemort, the chief antagonist of the series.

More Info

Lord Voldemort

He is the main antagonist in J. K. Rowling's series of Harry Potter novels. Nearly every witch or wizard dares not utter his name and refers to him instead with "You-Know-Who," "He Who Must Not Be Named," etc.

More Info

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is the character who is popularly called as "The Boy Who Lived" in the wizarding world. He is one of the most powerful, and red-headed wizards of the Hogwards school, who killed Voldemort.

More Info

Hermione Granger

She is one of the greatest witches of her age, who always uses her quick wit, deft recall, and encyclopaedic knowledge to lend aid in dire situations. Her best friends are Harry Potter and Ron Weasley. It's always a delight to talk to her.
